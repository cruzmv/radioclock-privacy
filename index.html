RadioClock Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

RadioClock does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The application uses Internet access only to retrieve and play publicly available radio stations.

RadioClock does not require user registration and does not create user accounts.

RadioClock does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking technologies.

No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

If future versions introduce new features involving personal data, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
